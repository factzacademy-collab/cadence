   216‚Üí   216‚ÜíSHARED DASHBOARD PRIMITIVES ‚Äî CONTRACT
   217‚Üí   217‚ÜíFile: `src/components/dashboard/shared.tsx`
   218‚Üí   218‚ÜíImport path: `@/components/dashboard/shared`
   219‚Üí   219‚ÜíAll primitives are client-safe ("use client" at the top of the file).
   220‚Üí   220‚Üí
   221‚Üí   221‚Üí1. PageHeader({ title, description?, actions?, className? })
   222‚Üí   222‚Üí   - Consistent view header. `title` is rendered inside an <h1>.
   223‚Üí   223‚Üí   - `actions` is a flex row of buttons/links on the right (wraps on
   224‚Üí   224‚Üí     mobile). Use this at the top of every view.
   225‚Üí   225‚Üí
   226‚Üí   226‚Üí2. StatCard({ label, value, delta?, deltaLabel?, icon?, accent?, spark? })
   227‚Üí   227‚Üí   - KPI card with a label, big value, optional delta percentage
   228‚Üí   228‚Üí     (positive = mint, negative = destructive), optional deltaLabel,
   229‚Üí   229‚Üí     optional lucide `icon` rendered in a tinted swatch (use `accent`
   230‚Üí   230‚Üí     like "text-primary", "text-coral", "text-mint", "text-plum"),
   231‚Üí   231‚Üí     and optional `spark: number[]` rendered as a MiniSparkline.
   232‚Üí   232‚Üí
   233‚Üí   233‚Üí3. SectionCard({ title?, description?, actions?, children?, className?, bodyClassName? })
   234‚Üí   234‚Üí   - Titled card wrapper used to group content inside a view. Header
   235‚Üí   235‚Üí     is omitted if none of title/description/actions are passed.
   236‚Üí   236‚Üí     `bodyClassName` defaults to `p-5`.
   237‚Üí   237‚Üí
   238‚Üí   238‚Üí4. StatusBadge({ status })
   239‚Üí   239‚Üí   - Colored Badge for PostStatus union:
   240‚Üí   240‚Üí     draft (muted), scheduled (primary), published (mint),
   241‚Üí   241‚Üí     failed (destructive), in-review (amber).
   242‚Üí   242‚Üí

   273‚Üí   273‚Üí

   275‚Üí   275‚Üí    - recharts PieChart donut. `color` per slice should be a CSS
